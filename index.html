Sakura River

Art by Waneella

Sakura River takes place in the fantasy world of Kakurokokorowombo, which is home to seven nations, each of which is tied to a different element and ruled by a different god. The story follows the Onii-chan, who has traveled across countless worlds with their twin sibling before becoming separated in Kakurokokorowombo. The Onii-chan travels in search of the lost sibling with their companion Stardust and becomes involved in the affairs of Kakurokokorowombo's nations.
